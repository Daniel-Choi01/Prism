Why Prism exists

A reflection instrument for the moments you're too close to see clearly.

When something is weighing on you, you're trapped inside one point of view — your own, in the middle of the feeling. Advice from others collapses everything into their single answer. Prism does the opposite: it takes what you wrote and splits it, like white light through glass, into the distinct perspectives already inside you — your future self, a mentor, a skeptic, your own values.

It never tells you what to do. It reflects, and it asks. The decision stays yours — you just get to see it whole first.

Powered by Claude (Anthropic). Your reflection is sent to a private endpoint, never shown to anyone else, and saved only if you choose to. Built for the SMG Bubble Cohort 001 “Reflection” Hackathon.

You're not alone in this

Prism is a tool for thinking — it can't be a person, and some moments need a person. These are real, free, and there for you any time.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline · US
Call or text 988, or chat at 988lifeline.org. Free and confidential, 24/7 — English & Spanish, with interpreters in 240+ languages.
Crisis Text Line · US
Text HOME to 741741 to reach a trained crisis counselor. Free, 24/7.
Outside the US — findahelpline.com
Free, confidential helplines in 130+ countries, in your language.
In immediate danger?
If you or someone near you is in immediate danger, call your local emergency number (911 in the US).

Prism is a student project — it can't contact anyone for you, but the services above are real and staffed by people who want to help.
